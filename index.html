Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Do I need any prior experience with Tarot or energy healing?
▾
Absolutely not. This workshop is designed to welcome complete beginners. All you need is an open mind and a willingness to explore. Coach Shivangi Kaur explains everything from first principles.
Is this workshop really free? What's the catch?
▾
Yes, completely free. There's no catch. At the end of the workshop, Coach Shivangi Kaur will share details about her deeper paid programs for those who want to go further — but that's entirely optional and there's zero pressure.
What do I need to attend the workshop?
▾
Just a stable internet connection, a device with Zoom installed, and a quiet comfortable space. A journal and pen are recommended. You do not need a Tarot deck for this workshop.
Will there be a recording if I can't attend live?
▾
We strongly recommend attending live for the full interactive experience and live readings. A replay may be made available briefly to registered participants — we'll notify you by email after the event.
Is Tarot related to religion or occult practices?
▾
Not at all. Tarot is a tool for self-reflection and intuition development. Coach Shivangi Kaur approaches it as a psychological and spiritual mirror — completely non-religious and accessible to people of all faiths and backgrounds.
How many people will be in the workshop?
▾
Seats are intentionally limited to maintain the quality of the experience and ensure everyone gets personal attention during the live Q&A. We recommend registering early.
